Indiana University Sentence Database File-Naming Convention

IUS-GXX2YY
	where:
		IUS = Indiana University Sentence Database acronym
		G = Gender (F = female, M = male)
		XX = Sentence number
		2 = Medium speaking rate (these sentences only exist in medium)
		YY = Participant/talker number

Example: IUS-M69201.wav
	IUS = Indiana University Sentence Database
	M = Male
	69 = Sentence 69
	2 = Medium speaking rate
	01 = Participant/talker #1
	
Example: IUS-F15217.wav
	IUS = Indiana University Sentence Database
	F = Female
	15 = Sentence 15
	2 = Medium speaking rate
	17 = Participant/talker #17
